### **CoworkerAndMe — Short Brief**

### **Value Proposition**

CoworkerAndMe is an **enterprise AI workspace** that lets employees safely use AI at work, while giving companies full control over **security, compliance, governance, and usage**.

Think **TypingMind / ChatGPT UI for Enterprise**, but with built-in **HR rules, auditability, and enterprise-grade guardrails**.

It enables teams to benefit from AI without risking data leaks, policy violations, or uncontrolled usage.

> **Safe AI for employees. Control for companies.**
> 

---

### **Target Customer**

- Mid-size and large companies (100–10,000+ employees)
- Regulated industries:
    - Finance
    - Healthcare
    - Insurance
    - Government / public sector
    - Consulting / legal
    - Manufacturing / enterprise SaaS
- Companies where:
    - ChatGPT is already used unofficially
    - IT / HR / Legal want control

    - Compliance requires audit logs

Buyer roles:

- CISO / Security
- Head of IT
- HR / Compliance / Legal

---

### **Key Features**

**1. Enterprise AI Chat Workspace**

- ChatGPT-style UI for employees
- Supports multiple models (OpenAI, Anthropic, local, Azure, etc.)
- Company knowledge integration (RAG)

**2. AI Governance & Guardrails**

- Prompt logging & audit trail
- Role-based permissions
- Data redaction / PII filtering
- Model access control
- Policy enforcement

**3. HR & Compliance Layer**

- Usage rules per department
- Prevent sensitive data leaks
- Track who used AI for what
- Export reports for audits
- GDPR / SOC2 / ISO-friendly logs

**4. Enterprise Security**

- SSO / SAML / SCIM
- On-prem / VPC / private cloud
- Encryption & access control
- No data sent to public APIs without approval

**5. IT Admin Console**

- Manage users & roles
- Approve tools / models
- Monitor usage
- Cost tracking
- Policy configuration

**6. AI App / Agent Hub**

- Internal AI tools
- Custom assistants
- Workflow agents
- Department-specific copilots
