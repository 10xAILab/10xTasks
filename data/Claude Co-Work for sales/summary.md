**Brief description:** A tour of Claude Co-Work for sales: installing Anthropic’s sales plugin, using custom “skills” for prospecting (e.g. LinkedIn post engagers), CRM mining, call prep, scheduled tasks, and analytics (win/loss, rep performance, pipeline review)—with emphasis on connectors like Apify, CRM, Gmail, calendar, and Fireflies.

### Co-Work, skills, and plugin overview (00:00 - 01:30)
Introduces Co-Work in the Claude desktop app, folder access, connectors, and skills; Anthropic’s sales plugin ships starter skills (account research, call prep, etc.), while the speaker’s team built a deeper custom plugin.

### Prospecting skill demo (LinkedIn engagers) (01:30 - 03:01)
Runs a `/` skill to scrape engagers from a LinkedIn post, qualify against ICP, enrich, and export CSV—illustrating slash-invoked skills for repeatable workflows.

### Key connectors for prospecting (03:01 - 04:31)
Recommends Apify for social scraping, plus Apollo/Clay/VIP Prospecting for data, Instantly/Uniow for outreach, CRM, Fireflies, calendar, and email.

### Building a skill from a live workflow (04:31 - 06:02)
Walks through doing the workflow once with Claude, correcting steps (e.g. use Apify for profile data), then packaging the markdown skill with numbered steps and scraper IDs.

### CRM “prospect miner” + sub-agents (06:02 - 07:32)
Demonstrates mining stale CRM leads with research, prioritization, and comms history; highlights parallel sub-agents for large lists to preserve context and speed.

### Sub-agent guidance and skill packaging (07:32 - 09:03)
Notes you must explicitly ask for sub-agents on bulk work; saves the CRM mining flow as a skill.

### Call prep skill + customization (09:03 - 10:33)
Runs call prep pulling CRM, emails, Fireflies, and web research into an HTML brief; explains customizing Anthropic’s base skills by prompting Claude with your format.

### Scheduled tasks + sales analytics (10:33 - 12:04)
Schedules morning call-prep batches; demos win/loss analysis across CRM columns with transcripts and emails exported to a detailed Google Doc.

### Rep performance + pipeline review (12:04 - 13:35)
Covers a rep scorecard skill and a pipeline review that can DM Slack summaries—optionally extending to pipeline hygiene updates from comms data.

### Closing pitch for AI accelerator (13:35 - 15:05)
Recaps connector importance, encourages experimenting with Co-Work/skills, and promotes the speaker’s accelerator, workshops, and Q&A.
