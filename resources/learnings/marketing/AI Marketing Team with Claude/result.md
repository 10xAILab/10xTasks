Title: Claude Code: Build Your Full AI Marketing Team (Agents + Claude Skills)

Brief description: Grace Leung walks through a practical system for building a multi-agent marketing workflow in Claude Code, from project setup to reusable skills and campaign execution. The tutorial focuses on turning common marketing tasks into structured, repeatable AI operations.

### System Overview and Four-Step Plan (00:00 - 02:55)
Grace introduces the goal of turning Claude into a full AI marketing team and outlines a four-step build process. She frames the approach for non-technical users and emphasizes mapping real weekly marketing tasks first.

### Project Setup and CLAUDE.md Foundations (02:55 - 05:50)
The video covers initializing a project in VS Code, creating a `CLAUDE.md` instruction layer, and organizing brand context files. This setup establishes shared rules and references so all future agents and skills behave consistently.

### Building Reusable Skills and Templates (05:50 - 08:40)
Grace demonstrates converting recurring workflows into Claude skills and connecting them to existing templates and SOPs. She shows how this speeds up deck creation and campaign documentation while preserving consistent formatting.

### Social Creative Skill and Style Libraries (08:40 - 11:30)
She builds a social design skill that uses a curated style library to generate branded assets like carousel posts. The section highlights controlling visual quality through references and prompts instead of ad hoc generation.

### Analytics Agent and Reporting Workflows (11:30 - 14:20)
The tutorial moves into data workflows, where an analyst agent produces interactive summaries, trend views, and channel-level performance insights. Grace positions this as a time-saving layer for repeat reporting and decision support.

### Orchestration: Agents vs Skills in Campaigns (14:20 - 17:31)
In the final section, she explains when to use full agents versus lightweight skills and demonstrates an end-to-end campaign flow. The takeaway is to reserve agents for complex synthesis tasks and use skills for direct execution tasks.
