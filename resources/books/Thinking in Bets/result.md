Title: Thinking in Bets by Annie Duke - A Visual Summary

Brief description: The video visualizes Annie Duke's decision framework: evaluate choices by process quality, not just outcomes. It emphasizes probability thinking, calibration, and learning loops for decisions under uncertainty.

### Key discussion segment 1 (00:00 - 03:15)
Introduces the core idea that life is probabilistic, so decisions should be treated as bets rather than certainties.

### Key discussion segment 2 (03:15 - 06:30)
Explains the mistake of "resulting"—judging decisions only by outcomes instead of the quality of reasoning at decision time.

### Key discussion segment 3 (06:30 - 09:45)
Shows how probabilistic language improves clarity, helping people communicate confidence levels and uncertainty more honestly.

### Key discussion segment 4 (09:45 - 13:00)
Discusses how groups can improve judgment through structured disagreement, accountability, and shared decision review.

### Key discussion segment 5 (13:00 - 16:16)
Ends with implementation tactics such as decision journaling and postmortems to improve long-term calibration.
