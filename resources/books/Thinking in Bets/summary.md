**Book:** Thinking in Bets: Making Smarter Decisions When You Don't Have All the Facts
**Author:** Annie Duke — A former professional poker player and decision strategist focused on probabilistic thinking.
**Amazon UK Kindle:** [Thinking in Bets on Amazon UK](https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/B074DG9LQF) — £5.99; Kindle Unlimited availability not confirmed.

**Brief description:** Duke teaches decision-making under uncertainty by separating decision quality from outcome quality. Using poker analogies, she shows how probability thinking improves judgment when evidence is incomplete.

## Key Insights
- Good decisions can have bad outcomes, and bad decisions can appear successful by luck.
- Probabilistic language improves calibration and reduces overconfidence.
- Feedback loops and decision journals build better long-term judgment.
