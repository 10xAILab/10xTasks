**Brief description:** `Superforecasting` studies why some people consistently make better predictions in uncertain domains. The book distills habits like probabilistic thinking, active open-mindedness, and frequent belief updating.

## Key Insights
- Forecasting skill can be trained through disciplined process and feedback.
- Top forecasters use probabilities, ranges, and continuous updates instead of binary certainty.
- Decomposing big questions into smaller ones improves predictive accuracy.
